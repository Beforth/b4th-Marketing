URS Evaluation Guidelines

Requirement Details: Capture all functional and non-functional requirements clearly.

Technical Specifications: Include dimensions, materials, performance criteria, and standards.

Site Requirements: Document installation space, power requirements, environmental conditions.

Timeline: Be realistic about project duration and milestones.

Budget Range: Provide a reasonable range based on project scope and complexity.
